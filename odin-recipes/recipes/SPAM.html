BBQ SPAM Over Rice

Description

SPAM has been an American pantry staple since the early 1900's when it was an affordable food during the Great Depression. Nowadays, it can easily be elevated to the next level with a bit of creativity and some very basic ingredients

Ingredients

  1. One can of regular SPAM
  2. One sweet yellow onion
  3. Garlic
  4. Mushrooms
  5. Rice
  6. BBQ sauce of choice

Steps

  • Boil the amount of rice necessary for the amount of people being served and set to the side (to elevate this further, wash your rice thoroughly before boiling to create sticky rice
  • Slice SPAM into strips that will all fit into a pan at the same time, then fry them (without oil) until crispy on all sides, then dice them into small squares
  • Dice the mushrooms, onion, and garlic before adding oil to the same pan and saut√© until soft (onions should be almost translucent)
  • Add the SPAM pieces and BBQ sauce into the vegetables and stir to combine
  • Serve mixture over rice
