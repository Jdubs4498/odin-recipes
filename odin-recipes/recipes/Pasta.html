Summer Pasta Salad

Description

Everyone loves something cool and delicious during a hot Summer day, enter a fresh pasta salad

Easy to make, relatively cheap, and always a hit for both family and parties

Ingredients

  1. Pasta of Choice (Fusilli is my personal favorite for this recipe)
  2. Mozzarella Pearls
  3. Cherry Tomatoes
  4. Full Bottle of Italian Dressing

Steps

  • Boil the pasta til al dente, drain, and set aside to cool
  • Halve the cherry tomatoes (feel free to go smaller if you don't like larger tomatoes)
  • Add pasta, tomatoes, and mozzarella pearls to a large mixing bowl before pouring the Italian dressing on and mixing thoroughly
  • Serve immediately if desired, though resting it in the fridge until dinner time will help make it that much cooler
